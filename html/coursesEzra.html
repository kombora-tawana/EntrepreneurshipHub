BUSINESS ACUMEN

BUSINESS ACUMEN :


Business acumen is a person's ability to understand business problems. It is a combination of general knowledge and organization-specific knowledge about how and why work is done. This is an important leadership quality and is reflected in the questions one asks and the decisions one makes, this also means seeing the bigger picture and recognizing that every decision, no matter how small, can affect your bottom line.
